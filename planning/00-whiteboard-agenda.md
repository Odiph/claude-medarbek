# Whiteboard Session Agenda — Thursday, 4:00 PM

**Attendees:** Tejas, Jason, Odi
**Duration:** ~90 min
**Facilitator:** Odi
**One outcome we must leave with:** a single written problem statement + a single use case chosen to test first, with 3–5 assumptions ranked by risk.

> Rule for the room: we are choosing *what to test*, not *what to build*. If a discussion turns into a feature spec, park it.

---

## 0. Frame (5 min)
- Restate the goal above. Read the operating principles aloud.
- Confirm the constraint: **$100k revenue before we allow ourselves to pivot or add use cases.**

## 1. Problem candidates — diverge (15 min)
- Each person writes problems (not solutions) on stickies, silently, 5 min.
- Round-robin read-out, cluster on the board.
- Prompt: *"Whose hair is on fire, and what does it cost them today?"*

## 2. Pick the target customer & problem — converge (20 min)
- Score each cluster on: **pain intensity · frequency · reachability · willingness to pay.** (See scoring grid in [01](./01-problem-definition-pathway.md).)
- HOA management is our leading candidate — pressure-test it against the others; don't just default to it.
- **Output:** one problem statement written in the template.

## 3. Use case & the OST top of tree (20 min)
- Write the **desired outcome** (the metric that improves if we win).
- Branch 2–3 **opportunities** (unmet needs) under it.
- Pick **one opportunity** to pursue first. Sketch 1–2 solution ideas — enough to test, not to build.

## 4. Riskiest assumptions (15 min)
- List every assumption the chosen use case depends on.
- Plot on the **assumption map**: desirability / viability / feasibility × how sure are we.
- Circle the top 3–5 that are *most risky and least known*. Those are what interviews and the pilot must attack.

## 5. Revenue hypothesis (10 min)
- For the chosen use case, pick the model to test first: **direct-pay B2C/B2B · freemium · sponsor-supported widget.**
- Note who pays, for what friction, and the smallest signal that would confirm willingness to pay.

## 6. Commitments & homework (5 min)
- Assign owners and dates from [02-homework.md](./02-homework.md).
- Confirm the daily HOA interview cadence and who books them.
